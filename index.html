About Me

Hello and welcome!

This is the digital corner of Zil Sheth — marketer, writer and storyteller.

Explore my work, learn my journey, or just stay to read my stories :)

Before Canada

I grew up in Vadodara, Gujarat (India), where I completed my Bachelor's in Business Administration and started working in marketing and SEO content writing early in my career. Around that time, I had plans to move to Canada for further studies — but like most plans in 2020, COVID had other ideas.

Instead of waiting around, I decided to keep building. I pursued a part-time postgraduate program in Marketing Management at Maharaja University of Baroda while working full-time as a marketing professional. I gained hands-on experience in social media campaigns, content strategy, and digital marketing projects.

After the Move

In 2023, I finally made the move to Canada and continued my education with two postgraduate programs:

  • Business Analytics — Seneca College
  • Content & Digital Marketing — York University

Now I'm based in Ontario, constantly learning, experimenting, and exploring how brands can connect with people in more meaningful ways. Currently, I work at Lanvac Surveillance, where I handle high-volume customer interactions and problem-solving in fast-paced situations. While it might not sound like marketing on paper, it has strengthened some of the skills that great marketers rely on every day:

  • Clear communication under pressure
  • Quick decision-making
  • Understanding human behaviour

And at its core, marketing is really about understanding people. My biggest interests lie in digital strategy, storytelling, audience psychology, and building brands that feel human rather than transactional. This portfolio is where I share the projects, campaigns, and ideas that reflect that journey.
