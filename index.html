About Me

Hello and welcome!

My name is Zil Sheth. I'm a results-driven marketing professional based in Toronto, specialising in content strategy, social media management, and campaign execution — with a proven ability to drive measurable outcomes, including 25% growth in user engagement and 20% increase in organic traffic.

I grew up in Vadodara, Gujarat, where I completed my Bachelor of Business Administration and started building my marketing career early — writing SEO content, running social campaigns, and figuring out how to make brands actually connect with people.

When COVID delayed my Canada move, I didn't wait around. I completed a postgraduate in Marketing Management while working full-time at a startup agency — and was promoted to Marketing Head within 6 months of joining.

In 2023, I finally made the move. Since then I've completed Business Analytics at Seneca College and Content & Digital Marketing at York University.
