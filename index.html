About Me

Hello and welcome!

This is the digital corner of Zil Sheth — marketer, writer, and storyteller.

Explore my work, learn my journey, or just stay to read a few stories :)

Before Canada

I grew up in Vadodara, Gujarat (India), where I completed my Bachelor's in Business Administration and began my career in marketing and SEO content writing.

I had plans to move to Canada for further studies — but like most plans in 2020, COVID had other ideas.

Instead of pausing, I kept building. I pursued a postgraduate program in Marketing Management while working full-time, gaining hands-on experience in social media campaigns, content strategy, and digital marketing projects.

After the Move

In 2023, I moved to Canada and continued my education through two postgraduate programs:

  • Business Analytics — Seneca College
  • Content & Digital Marketing — York University

Now based in Ontario, I'm constantly learning, experimenting, and exploring how brands can connect with people in more meaningful ways.

I currently work at Lanvac Surveillance, where I manage high-volume customer interactions and solve problems in fast-paced environments.

While it may not look like marketing at first glance, it has strengthened the skills that great marketing relies on:

  • Clear communication under pressure
  • Quick decision-making
  • Understanding human behaviour

At its core, marketing is about understanding people.

My interests lie in digital strategy, storytelling, audience psychology, and building brands that feel human rather than transactional.

This portfolio is where I share the work, ideas, and projects that reflect that journey.
